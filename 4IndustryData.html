Skills and Jobs

Over the next 6 months we aim to have a refined rendition of our app, with both clue-keeping, scorekeeping, and card management available. It will be up on both Google play, and Apple app stores, with plenty of consumer feedback rolling in. In this time as we continue to develop our existing service, we will look into hiring new additions to the team to aid us. This will be especially crucial as we plan to extend our services into other games. The main roles we would be looking to add to our team are as follows.

Project Manager

Our project manager will manage communication between team members, ensuring that everyone is on the same page. This position will involve direct communication with employees as they will organise workflow between staff members, coordinate meetings, and maintain a comfortable work environment. Experience with leadership and management techniques will be needed for this position, as you’ll be taking the head of meetings, and ensuring that everyone completes their work by the specified deadline.

Junior Game Programmer

Our junior game programmer will work in direct support of the Brogrammer. Their role will be to write and implement graphical and functional elements of code into the app. The ability to be self-motivated and have strong technical problem-solving skills will be essential to be able to interact with the already established team. As the development of the app uses C# & C++ languages, are proficiency in these areas will be essential.

Security Engineer

The safety of our consumers’ data is one of our top priorities in Ex Machina. As a Chief Security Engineer, you’ll be in charge of implementing security solutions within our products as you conduct risk assessments and identify/eliminate vulnerabilities in our app. If you have experience with a variety of security products such as firewalls and virus protection, and are ready to work in a fast-paced business environment, this is the job for you.

Creative Director

This is a key position in bringing this project to life, as you'll be the deciding factor in everything from colour palettes to font choice. If you think that image should be shifted a few pixels to the right, then it’s your call to make! You’ll be responsible in setting an appropriate aesthetic to match our vision, ensuring that a high-quality product is produced, that is accessible for all audiences. We’re looking for someone who’s proficient in creative design to elevate our brand; bonus points if you’ve got experience with the Adobe Creative Suite.
