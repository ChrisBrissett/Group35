Roles

We as Ex Machina, have decided that we will be defining roles for each of our members. This is because we all have very specific skills that we already excel at and have skills that we’d like to improve upon. In addition, we believe that dedicated roles in this project will help to give a clear and concise direction for each member to focus on.

Social Sensei (Product Manager) - Rich

A product manager’s role is to focus on the planning and execution of the overall product being delivered. From the start of conceptualisation, to the finished product, this role covers all areas to confirm that the product will be delivered in a timely and cost-effective manner. The highest priority for this role is to keep the product on track and aligned with the company’s goals and morals.

Brogrammer (Game Designer) - Chris

You’ll be working with your fellow team members to design the core aspects of the game and be a key member in bringing our vision to life. This position will involve working closely with our creative and software team as you design the core aspects of the game, and they implement your ideas into reality. You’ll be taking care of the logistical side of things, designing the graphics, animations and layout for our app. You need to be an innovative thinker, and quick to adapt to new situations.

Technical Evangelist (Software Technical Lead) - Paul

The technical lead role is crucial in developing a successful product and comprises of bringing together and overseeing the team of technical personnel. As with any lead position one of the most challenging part of developing is the co-ordination of different creative aspects. Specifically focusing on the development our application from a software and creative perspective gives rise to a lot of challenging problems that can call for some creative solutions.

Director of First Impressions (QA & UX Manager) - Kayla

The application we’re developing is ultimately to serve the user and their goals. Therefore, the user experience is our top priority. In order to achieve what we would consider the optimal user experience, one of the most important things is a bug-free application. This is where quality assurance and testing come into play. As QA & UX Manager I will ensure throughout every stage, our app is being tested for the best quality and experience. This also means organising testing groups and any alphas, betas, etc.

Comic Sans Annihilator (Media Coordinator) - Bernadette

As Media Coordinator, you’ll take full control of the creation and content posted to Ex Machina’s social media accounts, along with the creation of a brand package that can be used by any other content creators or businesses. If you’re someone who has experience with copywriting, an eye for graphic design, and are interested in taking the helm of an entire brand/application, this is the position for you. You’ll interact closely with the Creative Team to digitally grow our brand by identifying our audience and creating content to appeal to them. You’ll also be assisting the creative team with creating assets for our product.
