Team Profiles

16 Personalities Results

Regarding the MBTI profiles, as a team, we have a good combination of consulting and feeling types balanced by some opinionated, campaigner and debater types.

With half of the group falling into the (X)NFP category, they are more suited to interpersonal relationships and being in tune with everyone's feelings. In comparison, the rest of us with thinking and judging types are more suited to forging the path and motivating the team.

Learning Styles Results

Our learning styles are also complimentary; all 6 of us have the 'Pragmatist' approach as one of the highest scores we received. Being a pragmatic leaning group, we will thrive on group discussions and brainstorming.

Big 5 Results

Our big 5 test results are varied and cover all the bases once again. Everyone scored above average or average in at least one category, and we have members excelling in different traits. With this information we can use it as an aid when thinking of assigning tasks; we can first assess each other's strengths and weaknesses.
