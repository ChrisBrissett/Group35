IT WORK - DAMIAN ZINNI

Practice Manager - Vectra Corporation Limited

Vectra has mainly three areas of business:

  • Managed services
  • Security managements and consultation (GRC - Governance Risk and Compliance)
  • Reselling and implementing software and hardware

Vectra controls operations in Adelaide (head office), Sydney, Melburne, Perth, and also had penetration testers in Brisbane along with a sister firm in Singapore.



How long have you been working for this business?

"4.5 years."

Have you changed roles during your time of employment?

"Three times. Started as an intern (3 months), cybersecurity analyst (3 years), cybersecurity services manager (1 year to 1 and a half)."

What is your current role in the company and what does it entail?

"Practice Manager – I lead a security operations centre that has several cybersecurity analysts around Australia. I also manage implementations, provide level two support to analysts, and conduct presales (demos and presentations for prospective clients).

Part of my role is to collect data from different systems to define anomalies."

What kinds of clients/customers do you work with?

"A good range of customers including insurance, big banks, corporate and departmental, government, airlines, secure hosting services and charities/not-for-profit."

Do you travel around a lot? If yes, is this frequent?

"Not now, but before, I travelled a fair amount, depending on the quarter. Sometimes, I would be travelling 3 or 4 times a month or even more, due to clients being interstate."

What do you find most enjoyable about your job?

"Two things – going after new clients and providing a proof of concept or a demo. Other one, is more technical, involves understanding data from the system, and determining whether it’s malicious."

What do you find most challenging about your job?

"Working with people. Those demo/client engagements are the most challenging part. I have been conducting demos for the last two days, working with people from the company and with clients. There is a lot of preparation involved in a two-day demonstration."

Would you recommend your position to other people?

"Yes, but it depends on what you like doing. If you don’t enjoy talking to clients and managing their expectations, this isn’t the best role for you."

What is the ratio of working/talking to clients and talking to your own team?

"It depends on leads. For most of the year, it’s 50/50, but right now, there’s a high volume of new clients and opportunities, shifting to more 70/30; mostly talking to clients."

Would you say that most of your time is spent maintaining a system already in place, or developing?"

"Now, it's maintaining, two years ago it was the opposite."
